About EdVise

Empowering students to make informed career decisions through personalized guidance and comprehensive information about government educational opportunities.

Our Mission

To bridge the information gap between students and government educational opportunities by providing personalized career guidance, comprehensive college databases, and scholarship information tailored to individual needs and categories.

Our Vision

To create an inclusive digital platform where every Indian student, regardless of their background or location, has access to accurate, up-to-date information about educational opportunities and career paths.

Multi-Role Portal

Separate dashboards for students, parents, and teachers with role-specific features and monitoring capabilities.

Scholarship Matcher

AI-powered scholarship recommendations based on category (SC/ST/OBC/General), academic performance, and family income.

Career Simulator

What-if scenario tool that shows career outcomes based on stream choices, helping students make informed decisions.
